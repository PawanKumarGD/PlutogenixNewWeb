About Plutogenix™

India’s first breed-smart veterinary diagnostics lab.

Plutogenix™ brings you a hybrid of science and service with the same accuracy, speed, and care you expect in human diagnostics, now in animals.

Backed by General Diagnostics, a leading diagnostics solutions company, with 34 labs across India and a global footprint in 5 countries, we bring together world-class quality and Indian veterinary insight.

Mission And Vision

Our Mission

To provide accurate, affordable, and accessible veterinary diagnostic solutions for diverse species of animals across the world.

Our Vision

To develop the world's first breed-specific and species-specific veterinary diagnostics database with regional benchmarks for all animals globally to enable preventive, personalised and personalized treatment so animals can live healthier and fuller lives.
